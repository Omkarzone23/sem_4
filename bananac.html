Banana Coconut Idli

A delicious, sweet twist to the classic idli, banana and coconut idli is a quick and easy recipe to try for you next morning meal.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

30 min.

serving

2 servings

instructions

step 1

For banana idli, mix all ingredients.

step 2

Grease an idly tray with butter, spoon some batter into the moulds and steam till done. Remove from mould once done.

step 3

For jaggery sauce, heat coconut milk and add jaggery powder. Stir till dissolved. Serve warm idlis with warm coconut sauce.

ingredients

1 cup idli

4 tbsp jaggery

pinch of salt

A pinch of cardamom powder

1/2 ripe banana, chopped

1 cup coconut milk

2 tbsp jaggery powder for jaggery cream
