Baked Cheesy Potato And Leeks Soup

A hearty blend of baked potatoes, garlic and leeks combined with the flavours of butter, cheese and black pepper in vegetable stock..

prep time

10min.

cook time

25 min.

serving

3 servings

instructions

step 1

Peel the potatoes and cut them into big cubes. Season with salt and pepper and bake at 200*C for 15 minutes. Remove and keep a side .Heat butter in a pan, add chopped garlic and sauté for a couple of minutes. Add chopped leeks and cook till the leeks are soft.

step 2

Add in the baked potatoes and toss them together. Turn off the heat and let it cool down. Transfer to a food processor and blend to a smooth paste using a little vegetable stock if needed .Heat butter in a pan and transfer the blended potato and leek purée to the pan. Add vegetable stock and stir well.

step 3

Season with salt and crushed black pepper. Add in the cheese and let it simmer till the cheese melts .Finish with chopped parsley. Serve hot.

ingredients

2 pcs potato large

50 gms leeks

3 garlic pods,peeled

to taste salt,to taste black pepper (crushed)

40 gms processed cheese

2 cups vegetable stock

2 tbsp butter1 tbsp parsley
