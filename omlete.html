Chilli Garlic Omlette

As the name suggests, this omelette is made with fiery chilli and piquant garlic flavours. If you are someone with spicy taste palettes, this recipe is a worthwhile attempt.

prep time

05 min.

cook time

10 min.

serving

2 servings

instructions

step 1

Break two eggs in a glass, whisk it properly.

step 2

Once done add chopped garlic, salt, chilli flakes and red chilli powder.

step 3

Mix everything again.

step 4

Heat a dollop of butter in a pan, pour the whisked egg batter, flip and cook the omelette from both sides.

step 5

You can also add a slice of cheese to give it a balanced taste.

step 6

Serve and enjoy!

ingredients

2 Eggs

1/2 tsp Chilli Flakes

To taste Red Chilli Powder

Salt To Taste

3-4 Cloves Garlic (Chopped)
