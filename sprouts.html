Mixed Sprouts Corn Chaat

A chaat recipe that you can prepare in a matter of few minutes with simple ingredients. A healthy, sumptuous and chatpata bowl of both taste and nutrients.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

20 min.

serving

2 servings

instructions

step 1

Soak the sprouts overnight and next morning pressure cook it for around 10-15 minutes. Strain well.

step 2

Now in as bowl, combine all the ingredients together and serve. Simple to prepare, easy to consume.

ingredients

15 gms mixed sprouts, boiled15 gms corn, boiled

1 tomato, finely chopped1 onion, finely chopped

1 tbsp coriander chutney

1/2 tsp cumin powder1/2 tsp red chilli powder or paprika

to taste Salt

Handful of pomegranate seeds

Handful of coriander leaves, chopped
