Beetroot Pulao

This refreshing drink helps in detoxification and keep your body cool during the summers.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

2 servings

instructions

step 1

Soak rice in water for at least half an hour.

step 2

Fry cumin seeds in hot oil; saute onions, green chilli and ginger-garlic paste.

step 3

Saute tomatoes, salt and all the spices.

step 4

Add chopped beetroot and potatoes and rice. Add water and let it all cook. Serve hot.

ingredients

2 Onions, sliced

2 Tomatoes chopped,2 Potatoes chopped

1 small Beetroot, finely chopped1 cup Rice

1 tbsp Ginger-garlic paste

1 Green chilli, slit

1/2 tsp Cumin Seeds

1/4 tsp Turmeric powder
