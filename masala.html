Masala Oats Soup

A wholesome and yummy breakfast, brimming with the goodness of oats and flavours of chilli, ginger and garlic!

prep time

10 min.

cook time

10 min.

serving

2 servings

instructions

step 1

To begin with the recipe, take 1 cup of oats and roast them until crispy. Once done, keep aside.

step 2

Heat ghee in a pan, add cumin seeds and let them splutter. Then add ginger-garlic paste and saute for a minute.

step 3

Then add chopped onion and fry until translucent. Once done, add all the veggies and saute for 5-6 minutes.

step 4

Now add turmeric powder and salt. Mix well. Add garam masala, coriander powder, red chilli powder and mix again.

step 5

Pour water as required and then add roasted oats. Cover for 5-6 minutes.

step 6

Serve and enjoy!

ingredients

1 Cup Oats

1 small Onion (Chopped) 1 small Tomato (Chopped) 2 tbsp Carrot (Chopped)

2 Green Chillies

2 tbsp Peas

1/2 tsp Garam Masala

to taste Red Chilli Powder

1 tsp Turmeric Powder to taste Salt
