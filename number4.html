Veg Soup

With the temperature still low to make us want something warm and comforting, this veg corn soup will make for the perfect dinner meal.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

2 servings

instructions

step 1

It's simple. Cut and boil vegetables like beans, cabbage, carrots and broccoli with boiled American corn.

step 2

Then add 2 tsp oats flour in some water to make a slurry and add it to the vegetable and corn mixture.

step 3

Season with salt and black pepper. When the veggies are done, add garlic crushed, paneer cubes and steamed broccoli. Serve hot. Also Read: 5 Dal Soup Recipes For Your Weight Loss Diet)

ingredients

carrots

broccoli

cabbage

cottage cheese

1/2 tsp oats flour
