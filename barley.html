Barley Soup Recipe

A delicious soup recipe which is made from barley and goodness of crunchy veggies like cauliflower and beans.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

10 min.

serving

2 servings

instructions

step 1

Heat oil and add garlic, green chilli and onion to it. Saute well.

step 2

Add barley, masoor and rest of the vegetables. saute for a minute on medium flame.

step 3

Add salt, haldi and saute for a while.

step 4

Add water and pressure cook for up to 4 whistles.

step 5

Transfer it to a bowl, add ghee, black pepper and salt (if needed).

step 6

Serve hot.

ingredients

2 Tbsp barley, soaked for 2 hours 1 Tbsp masoor dal, soaked for 30 minutes

1 tsp oil 2 garlic cloves, crushed 1 green chilli, chopped

1 tsp onion, chopped 2 Tbsp carrot, cubed

2 Tbsp beans, cubed 2 Tbsp cauliflower, cubed

1/2 tsp haldi To taste Salt

2-3 water 1 tsp ghee

1/2 tsp black pepper powder
