Cucumber & Coriander Smoothie

This refreshing drink helps in detoxification and keep your body cool during the summers.

prep time

05 min.

cook time

10 min.

serving

2 servings

instructions

step 1

Take one cucumber and roughly chop it.

step 2

Add this to a blender with a cup of coriander leaves and some water. Blend it to make juice.

step 3

Now in a glass, add some pink salt and pepper with some ice cubes. Now pour in the prepared juice.

step 4

Drink it cold and fresh to enjoy!

ingredients

Cucumber

Coriander leaves

some water

pink salt
