Gajar Matar Khichdi

This easy and quick dish is as much a hit with the weekend special rich and robust curries, as it is with a quick weekday dal and achaar combination.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

20 min.

serving

2 servings

instructions

step 1

Soak dal and water together for about one hour.

step 2

In a pressure cooker, saute hing and cumin seeds in ghee. Once they crackle, add green chillies.

step 3

Add grated carrots and peas, salt, garam masala and turmeric powder. Saute for 2-3 minutes.

step 4

Add rice and dal, add water as per required consistency.

step 5

Cook and serve hot with a dollop of ghee and dahi or achaar.

ingredients

1 Cup Rice ,1/2 cup Moong Dal

2 Carrots (grated) ,1/2 cup Peas

A pinch of hing

Salt to taste, 1-2 Green Chillies

1/2 tsp Turmeric ,1 tsp Cumin Seeds

1 tbsp Garam MasalaGhee for cooking
